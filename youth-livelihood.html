Building a Future of Opportunity and Prosperity

At the Innovative Development and Empowerment Agency (IDEA), we recognize that youth are the driving force behind sustainable development and social change. With a significant portion of the global population comprising young people, investing in their livelihoods is crucial for economic growth, social stability, and the overall well-being of communities. Our Youth Livelihood programs are dedicated to empowering young people with the skills, resources, and opportunities they need to build prosperous and fulfilling futures.

Our Approach

Our approach to enhancing youth livelihoods is comprehensive and tailored to address the diverse challenges faced by young people in various contexts. We focus on creating enabling environments that foster innovation, entrepreneurship, and employment opportunities.

Development and Vocational Training:We provide market-relevant skills training and vocational education to equip youth with the competencies needed for gainful employment and entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship and Business Support:We nurture entrepreneurial spirit by offering mentorship, business development services, and access to finance, enabling youth to start and grow their own enterprises

Career Guidance and Employment Services:We offer career counseling, job placement services, and employability workshops to help youth navigate the job market effectively.

Life Skills and Leadership Development:We conduct training that enhances life skills, leadership abilities, and civic engagement, preparing youth to be proactive and responsible citizens

Partnerships and Collaboration:We collaborate with governments, private sector, educational institutions, and other stakeholders to create a supportive ecosystem for youth livelihood development.

Our Impact

The Innovative Development and Empowerment Agency (IDEA) has made a profound and lasting impact on youth and their communities through our dedicated livelihood programs. Our initiatives have empowered young people to break free from the cycle of poverty, develop sustainable livelihoods, and contribute meaningfully to their societies.

Increased Employment Opportunities: We have successfully trained over 300 young people in market-relevant skills, leading to significant increases in employment rates among program participants. Many of these youths have secured stable jobs in various industries, improving their financial independence and quality of life.

Enhanced Digital Literacy: Our digital literacy and innovation programs have equipped youth with the skills to thrive in the digital economy. This has opened up new opportunities in tech-driven sectors, from online businesses to digital marketing, helping to close the digital divide and prepare youth for the future of work.

Improved Social Inclusion: IDEA's programs have been particularly impactful in reaching marginalized and vulnerable youth, including those from rural areas, low-income families, and disadvantaged backgrounds. By offering tailored support, we have helped these young people overcome social and economic barriers, integrating them into the mainstream economy and society.

Promotion of Sustainable Practices: Our agripreneurship and vocational training programs emphasize sustainable practices, leading to the adoption of environmentally friendly methods in agriculture and other industries. As a result, we have seen improved productivity, reduced environmental impact, and enhanced food security in the communities where we work
