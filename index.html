Innovative Development and Empowerment Agency

Our mission is to create meaningful change in the lives of those who need it most, by addressing the root causes of inequality, advocating for environmental stewardship, and promoting health and well-being for all using the followwing intiatives.

Climate Justice

Our Climate Justice initiatives focus on combating climate change by supporting sustainable practices, educating communities, and advocating for policy changes.

Health

Our Health initiatives aim to provide accessible healthcare services, promote healthy lifestyles, and address public health challenges in underserved communities.

Youth Livelihood

Our Youth Livelihood programs are dedicated to empowering young people with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need to thrive in today’s economy

Civic Accountability

Our Civic Accountability initiatives are focused on promoting transparency, strengthening democratic institutions, and ensuring that citizens have a voice in governance.

Services

At IDEA, we offer a wide range of services designed to empower individuals, strengthen communities, and drive sustainable development. From civic education to legal aid, our services are tailored to meet the unique needs of those we serve, ensuring that every initiative we undertake contributes to a more just and equitable society.
